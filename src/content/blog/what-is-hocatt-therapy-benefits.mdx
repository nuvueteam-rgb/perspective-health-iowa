## What Is HOCATT?

HOCATT stands for Hyperthermic Ozone and Carbonic Acid Transdermal Technology. It's a personal steam sauna capsule that combines ten therapeutic modalities into one 30-minute session. Rather than scheduling separate appointments for ozone therapy, infrared therapy, and other treatments, the HOCATT delivers them all simultaneously.

The technology amplifies each modality by delivering them together. When your body is warmed, your pores open, circulation increases, and your tissues become more receptive to therapeutic agents. This synergy is what makes HOCATT so effective.

## The 10 Therapeutic Modalities

What sets the HOCATT apart from a standard sauna is the breadth of what it delivers in each session:

- **Transdermal Ozone** — Ozone (O3) is delivered through the skin via steam, supporting oxygenation, detoxification, and immune function.
- **Carbonic Acid** — CO2 is introduced early in the session to dilate blood vessels, enhancing the absorption of ozone that follows.
- **Whole Body Hyperthermia** — A controlled rise in core body temperature mimics a mild fever response, stimulating immune activity and healing.
- **Far Infrared** — Infrared energy penetrates deep into tissues, promoting circulation and reducing inflammation.
- **Steam Sauna** — Moist heat opens pores and encourages sweating, one of the body's most natural detox pathways.
- **Electrotherapy** — Mild microcurrents support pain management, muscle relaxation, and cellular energy production.
- **Aromatherapy** — Essential oils infused into the steam support relaxation and respiratory health.
- **Photon Light Therapy** — Specific light frequencies promote cellular repair and mitochondrial energy production.
- **Ultraviolet Irradiation** — UV light supports the body's natural defense systems.
- **Oxygen Breathing** — You breathe pure humidified oxygen throughout the session, boosting blood oxygen saturation.

## The Science Behind HOCATT

The foundation of HOCATT therapy rests on well-studied principles. Ozone therapy increases oxygen delivery to cells and tissues. When oxygen availability improves, cells produce energy more efficiently, metabolic waste is cleared more effectively, and immune function is supported.

Hyperthermia — the controlled elevation of body temperature — enhances these effects significantly. As your body warms, blood vessels dilate, circulation accelerates, and your skin becomes a more effective pathway for absorbing therapeutic agents. The modalities work synergistically, each amplifying the others, producing results beyond what any single treatment could achieve alone.

Research on individual components — including ozone therapy, far infrared, and hyperthermia — has shown promise in supporting immune modulation, reducing oxidative stress, and promoting natural detoxification.
